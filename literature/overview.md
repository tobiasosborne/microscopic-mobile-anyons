## Annotated entries (initial set)
- Etingof et al., *Tensor Categories* (2015) — standard reference for fusion categories; basis for formal definitions of simples, fusion, F/R symbols. PDF: `literature/pdfs/etingof_tensor_categories.pdf`. Status: `[unverified]`.
- Etingof, *Introduction to Fusion Categories* (2015, arXiv:1509.02921) — concise lecture notes; good for quick recall of axioms and examples. PDF: `literature/pdfs/etingof_intro_fusion_categories_2015.pdf`. Status: `[unverified]`.
- Kitaev (2006, cond-mat/0506438) — anyons in exactly solved model; key for physical intuition and diagrammatics. PDF: `literature/pdfs/kitaev_anyons_exact_model_2006.pdf`. Status: `[unverified]`.
- Feiguin et al. (2006, cond-mat/0612341) — “golden chain” interacting Fibonacci anyons; baseline dense-chain limit. PDF: `literature/pdfs/feiguin_golden_chain_2006.pdf`. Status: `[unverified]`.
- Trebst et al. (2008, arXiv:0801.4602) — collective states of Fibonacci anyons; phase structure for anyonic chains. PDF: `literature/pdfs/trebst_collective_states_fibonacci_anyons_2008.pdf`. Status: `[unverified]`.
- Leinaas, Myrheim (1977, quant-ph/0701097) — foundational identical-particle/anyon formalism; motivates first-quantised approach. PDF: `literature/pdfs/leinaas_myrheim_identical_particles_1977.pdf`. Status: `[unverified]`.
- Anyonic Fock space (2019, arXiv:1910.10619) — first-quantised anyonic Fock-space perspective; relevant for variable-N sectors. PDF: `literature/pdfs/anyonic_fock_space_2019.pdf`. Status: `[unverified]`.
- Anyonic Lieb–Liniger / Bethe ansatz (2007, arXiv:0704.1881) — exact solution for 1D anyons with delta interactions; informs dilute-limit modelling. PDF: `literature/pdfs/anyon_lieb_liniger_bethe_ansatz_2007.pdf`. Status: `[unverified]`.
- Tonks–Girardeau anyons (2009, arXiv:0903.5291) — Bethe-ansatz treatment of hard-core anyons; relevant for Girardeau mapping work. PDF: `literature/pdfs/anyon_tonks_girardeau_bethe_ansatz_2009.pdf`. Status: `[unverified]`.
- Girardeau (1960, J. Math. Phys. 1, 516) — "Relationship between Systems of Impenetrable Bosons and Fermions in One Dimension". Foundational paper for hard-core bosons. PDF: `literature/pdfs/girardeau_impenetrable_bosons_1960.pdf`. Status: `[unverified]`.
- Girardeau (2006, PRL 97, 100402) — "Anyon-Fermion Mapping and Applications to Ultracold Gases in Tight Waveguides". Generalization of Fermi-Bose mapping to anyons. PDF: `literature/pdfs/girardeau_anyon_fermion_mapping_2006.pdf`. Status: `[unverified]`.
- Etingof, Nikshych, Ostrik (2005, Annals of Math 162) — "On fusion categories". Proves general results about fusion categories in characteristic zero. PDF: `literature/pdfs/etingof_on_fusion_categories_2005.pdf`. Status: `[unverified]`.
- Etingof et al. (2009) — "Tensor Categories" (Lecture Notes). Precursor to the 2015 book. PDF: `literature/pdfs/etingof_tensor_categories_lectures_2009.pdf`. Status: `[unverified]`.
